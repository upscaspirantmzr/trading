Your Daily Trading Checklist

Follow these steps diligently to maximize your edge and manage your capital effectively.

Pre-Market Preparation

  • Review daily/15-min charts for overall trend, support/resistance.
  • Calculate daily pivot points.
  • Define max daily loss (e.g., ₹500 for 10k capital).
  • Define max daily profit target (e.g., ₹500-₹1000 for 10k capital).
  • Commit to only 1-2 trades today.

Market Open (First Trade)

  • Mark first minute candle high/low and 50% retracement.
  • Wait for retracement and confirmation candle.
  • Check option premium volume (and IV if possible).
  • Before Entry: Define precise entry, profit target, and stop loss for this specific trade (max ₹100-₹200 loss).
  • Enter the trade (1 lot only, or appropriate small size).
  • Place SL and Target immediately.
  • Practice "Set it and Forget it" and trailing SL if in profit.

After First Trade

  • If profitable and hits daily target: CLOSE TERMINAL. Enjoy your day.
  • If profitable but not daily target: Re-evaluate for a second trade based purely on price action, sticking to your risk rules.
  • If a loss: Review why. Do not take a revenge trade. If daily loss limit is hit, CLOSE TERMINAL.

End of Day Review

  • Update your trading journal meticulously (entry/exit, P&L, emotions, lessons).
  • Analyze your performance and emotions.
